              Hour of Code




Hour of Code is a one-hour introduction to computer science, with a goal of showing that anyone is capable of learning the basics and to broaden their knowledge on how computer logic works. This program has now more than 400 partners and 200.000 teachers all over the world. Moreover, by the time of writing this, 1,126,263,227 served in this event in more than 180 countries globally. This year 45 events were registered in Azerbaijan.






















        How was our Hour of Code?




On December 12th, students of ADA University, Javid Alakbarli, Argun Samadov, Zahra Zeynalli, and Nazrin Zamanova hosted the “Hour of Code” event online with the students from Aghdam (Karabakh) Secondary School No.88.

Special thanks to Code.org for creating a platform for students to study, and for creating an “Hour of Code” for those who would like to teach for others.We had a great time and it was a pleasure to be a part of this community.

"Keeping the event active was the primary goal of ours, so we managed to complete it. Although we thought it would be challenging to surpass our initial thoughts, we did a great job in teaching as well as communicating with the students." - Web Team

Robotics is an interdisciplinary field that integrates computer science and engineering. Robotics involves design, construction, operation, and use of robots. The goal of robotics is to design machines that can help and assist humans.
